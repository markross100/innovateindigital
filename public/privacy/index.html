The AI tools on this site

This site showcases a small suite of personal AI tools — including Automation Manager, Process Prioritisation Matrix, and others. These tools are browser-based and designed with privacy in mind:

  • ✓
    Data stays on your device — anything you enter into these tools is stored only in your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device and is never transmitted to or stored on any server run by this site.
  • ✓
    No personal data required — the tools are designed to work with process and organisational information, not personal data. Please do not enter information that identifies individuals.
  • ✓
    AI processing — to generate outputs, tool inputs are sent to the Claude API, operated by Anthropic PBC. Anthropic does not use submitted data to train its models. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details.
  • ✓
    Your control — you can clear all locally stored tool data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.
