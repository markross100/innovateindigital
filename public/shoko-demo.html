Our Story

A place where moments become memories

SHOKO means "bright and joyful" in Japanese — and that spirit flows through every detail of our space, from morning light to evening candlelight.

We created SHOKO as a place where you can share unforgettable moments with family, loved ones, and friends. Homemade cakes and pastries, specialty teas and coffees, handcrafted cocktails, and curated Apéro Boards — there is always something for every mood.
